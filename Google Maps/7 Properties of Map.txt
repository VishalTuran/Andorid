Camera Position is the total view shown by map at a time on screen.

Target(What Camera Sees)-Defined by longitude and latitude, Center of map.

Zoom-What camera sees.

0 Very Far(Full World Map)

21 Very close (Even streets are visible)

Bearing-What direction camera is pointing. Specified in degrees clockwise in north.

If you want camera to face west bearing will be 90 degress.

Tilt:(How Much camera is tilted by)

By default its 0 meaning camera is Facing direcing towards ground.
